Hobbies?

A big part of my life has consisted of A R T. Mainly around creating "fine" art, ironic because there is nothing fine about my third-grade education and skills on art. My art mainly focuses on realistic portraits of people, ideally old people because they are more interesting to draw. My preferred mediums are dry and digital; I don't mess with wet mediums since they are significantly more expensive and we out here brokebois.

When I am not wasting my time and money drawing, I like to waste more money I do not have on adventure. My significant other and will usually just pick a random loaction that we havent been to that is sort of near by. No planning; just pack our bags and get on the road. For locations, any area surrounded by mothernature is priority.
